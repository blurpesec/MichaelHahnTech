About Me


         Approaching the end of my college education, I will have earned my bachelor's degree in information technology by the end of Spring, 2018. Until now, my skills have mainly been in windows-based systems administration and traditional troubleshooting roles, but I would like to expand out more into scripting and web application-based development. My main interest is technology as I believe that tech can help to provide for a better future for many of the people that have been forgotten in the past.

         After spending a large amount of time educating myself, I am of the belief that the Ethereum project is one of the projects that can most benefit humanity through its inclusion of, and collaboration with, a huge and diverse community of developers to provide a useable peer-to-peer ecosystem for decentralized applications. More specifically, I am interested in projects that help to educate and universalize security for non-tech-savvy users as I believe security and education (coupled with simplicity-of-use) will be some of the largest obstacles for Ethereum adoption at an international scale.

         To gain the knowledge needed to contribute to projects that interest me and benefit the future, I am pursuing interests in cryptography, mathematics, and web application-based software development during my last semester of school. I have begun teaching myself javascript and python as well as immersing myself in technical discussions about scaling solutions to learn relevant terminology.
